Hailing from down under, the Flat White is an Australian and New Zealand specialty that has captured the hearts of coffee enthusiasts worldwide. It boasts a double shot of espresso, complemented by velvety microfoam milk. With a higher coffee-to-milk ratio than a latte, the Flat White highlights the robust coffee flavor while caressing the palate with its creamy texture.
Ingredients: Freshly brewed double espresso, microfoam milk.
